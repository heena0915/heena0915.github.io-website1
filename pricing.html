Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to try Paperflite?

No credit card needed! Just give us your email address, and we’ll take it from there.

Can I stop the package at any point?

Yes, you can stop your plan at any point, without incurring any additional charges.

Can I change my package?

Of course! Upgrade anytime, and your next bill will reflect your new plan.

What happens when my trial period ends?

Your content, collections and customizations will all remain as is for one week, and we’ll contact you to see if you want to continue with a paid plan.

Can I extend my trial period?

Just ping us on our chatbox, and one of our team will get in touch to discuss this further.

How can I transfer my content offline?

Send us an email at support@paperflite.com, and our team will guide you through the process.
